Our Services

Verified Pets

Verification of pets is crucial to ensure their authenticity, health, and ethical treatment. It eliminates the risk of fraud, such as selling fake or unhealthy pets, and guarantees that the pets come from reputable sources. Verified information on a pet's origin, health records, and ownership history provides transparency and trust for buyers, promoting responsible pet adoption and fostering confidence in the pet ecosystem.

Pet health care

Petchain simplifies the management of your pet's health care needs by offering comprehensive scheduling for vaccinations, check-ups, and ongoing health monitoring, as well as access to reliable veterinary care.

Pet Market

Petchain hosts a versatile marketplace where users can buy and sell pets, as well as a wide range of pet products.

Pet Grooming

Petchain connects pet owners with nearby grooming salons or offers the option to book home grooming services.

Pet Community

Petchain fosters a vibrant community through various interactive features. Real-time chats allow pet owners to communicate with each other and service providers, sharing tips and advice.

Adoption Services

Petchain supports animal adoption by offering an extensive database of adoptable pets, including dogs, cats, birds, reptiles, and small furry creatures

Pet Socialisation

Petchain provides comprehensive solutions for dog training, walking, and social interaction. Professional trainers offer both in-person and virtual sessions, covering everything from basic obedience to advanced agility training, catering to all breeds and temperaments.
